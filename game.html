My Super Cool Game!

The Story

Once upon a time there was a purple block named Bobby. Bobby has always had a passion for bouncing.

Little did Bobby know there was an evil block named Bert who hates it when Bobby jumps. Bert's evil plan is to run into Bobby so Bobby will stop bouncing.

Bobby's not afraid of Bert so he keeps jumping! The more jumps Bobby gets in, the angrier Bert gets. The more angry Bert is, the faster he goes.

Instructions

The goal of the game is to get as many jumps in as possible without getting hit by Bert.

Use the space bar or click onto the screen to make Bobby jump! The more jumps, the more points you get. Click to Play!
